EYE & EYE PROBLEMS

Antioxidants are substances that guard the body from Oxidation and protect it from the damage of free radicals. Free radicals mean molecules with one or more unpaired electrons that can destroy cells and cause diseases. Antioxidant vitamins B, C and beta carotene and more help reduce the chances of macular degeneration and several other eye diseases.
Astigmatism is a refractive error. Objects at all distances appear distorted. An eye affected by astigmatism sees objects in stretched, streaked or elongated shapes and have to squint and apply strain to see properly.
Macular Degeneration: Macular Degeneration signifies the gradual changes in Eye's macula that result in the loss of central vision. Age Related Macular Degeneration is common and a leading cause of loss of eyesight.
Ultraviolet: The part of the light spectrum we cannot see is known as Ultraviolet light and these rays have shorter wavelengths. Both UVA and UVB light are damaging for the eyes as well as skin.
